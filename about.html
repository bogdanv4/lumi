Where It All Began

We began designing the beloved charms in 2000. Every pendant has a meaning, sometimes multiple meanings, one given to it by the designer and many more that it receives from the person who wears and loves it. Whether the focus is on color or pattern, or the pendant is dedicated to a country, occasion, activity or most importantly, a person, each pendant is designed to tell the wearer's personal story and showcase their unique style. Our charms are lovingly worn on bracelets and necklaces; they are specifically created to be worn in ways that are unique to the wearer.
